## Key Findings

1. Order volume showed month-over-month decline, confirming that revenue loss was volume-driven rather than traffic-driven.

2. Average Order Value (AOV) remained stable across months, ruling out pricing, discounting, or basket-size issues.

3. Payment failure rates were elevated but relatively stable, indicating a structural checkout issue rather than a recent regression.

4. Post-payment fulfillment analysis revealed a critical issue: 20–30% of successfully paid orders were not delivered.

5. Breakdown of non-delivered orders showed that returns were the largest contributor to post-payment revenue loss, followed by pre-delivery cancellations.
