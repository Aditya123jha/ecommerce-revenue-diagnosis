## Key Findings

1. Overall revenue declined despite stable traffic and consistent average order value (AOV).
2. Month-over-month order volume showed mild fluctuation but no structural drop.
3. Payment failure rate remained relatively stable (~7–10%) and did not explain revenue decline.
4. Post-payment fulfillment failures were high, with 20–30% of successfully paid orders not delivered.
5. Returned orders were the largest contributor to non-delivery, followed by pre-delivery cancellations.
6. Revenue leakage was primarily operational rather than demand-side or pricing-related.
