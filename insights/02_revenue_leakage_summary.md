## Revenue Leakage Summary

Despite stable traffic, consistent pricing, and successful payment completion, the platform experiences significant revenue loss after checkout.

Analysis shows that approximately 20–30% of successfully paid orders fail to convert into realized revenue due to post-payment fulfillment issues. The majority of this leakage is driven by returned orders, followed by pre-delivery cancellations.

Payment gateway failures and pricing-related factors were ruled out as primary causes, as both payment success rates and average order value remained stable during the period.

This indicates that the revenue decline is primarily driven by operational and logistics inefficiencies rather than demand-side, monetization, or acquisition issues. Improving fulfillment reliability represents the highest-impact lever for revenue recovery.
